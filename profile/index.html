Profile

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Nottingham. My research interests are in the field of time series econometrics, applied macroeconomics as well as industrial structural change.

I have previously studied at Duke University, the University of Manchester as well as the Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU) and hold master's degrees in economics and econometrics.

My professional experience includes an extensive and ongoing consultancy with the United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO), a position as research assistant in the field of economics of education as well as at the Social Science Research Institute (SSRI) at Duke.
